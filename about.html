About TransPic

HomeLanSync is a new way of share photos, we use the app between our family and friends for years, and they all give it a thumb up. Then, we think it is time to share the app with public. So, we release the app on this site.

But, as you know, this is a beta version of the app, we do not guarantee the app would solve all problems, but we are working on it continuosly. And, you are welcome to submit bug report to us.

Some features that might be added in the future. If you think you need some feature, please feel free to contact me and I should be able to figure it out.
