Features

The HomeLanSync includes:

  • Share in Home
  • Running in local area network in home only
  • No need connect to internet
  • One click share to home computer
  • Set password as your wish
  • Automatic find

Once installed, and configured, no future configered or operations on your computer or phone. Store all your pictures captured in home computer. The App automaticly find the home computer, and upload your pictures by one click.
